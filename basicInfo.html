Basic Info

  • Title
  • The Time Machine
  • Author
  • H.G. Wells
  • Published
  • Heinemann, 1895.
  • Genre
  • Adventure, Science Fiction
  • Setting
  • The novel is mainly set hundreds of thousands of years in the future when the Time Traveler takes a journey in his time machine.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is told from the first and third person point of view. The first when the narrator describes his meeting with the Time Traveller, and in the third person when we learn about the Time Traveller's journeys.
  • Conflict
  • One of the main conflicts in the novel is the Time Travelers encounters with the Morlocks. The Morlocks are dangerous, human like creatures that live deep underground in the future where the Time Traveler visits. They threaten his life and the other Elois lives and the Time Traveler must survive them in order to find his machine again.
