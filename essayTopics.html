Essay Topics

  • What are the Time Traveller's theories on how the Eloi function?
  • The Time Traveler believes that the Eloi are the only descendants of modern humanity, however as scientific developments continued to make life easier, they became more lazy and stupid.
  • He argues that communism is the cause of this as it has eliminated human competition and therefore made being lazy much simpler.
  • When the Time Traveller discovers the Morlocks he realizes that they must also be descendants of humanity as well.
  • He views the Morlocks as the working class that descended from the failures of capitalism.
  • He sees them as the slaves of the Eloi that have been so suppressed that they developed into a brute force that hunts the Eloi.
  • He views the hunting of the Eloi as an act of revenge for the Morlocks for the centuries of oppression as the overworked, underpaid working class.
  • Explain how the Morlocks live.
  • The Morlocks live deep under the surface of the earth in a system of tunnels.
  • The tunnels are accessed by a series of ventilation ducts that are scattered all across the land.
  • The Morlocks stay in the darkness, mostly living underground by day and outside at night, though some roam around outside in the shadows during the day.
  • The Morlocks spend the night hunting Eloi, as it is their food source, therefore the Eloi remain hidden in bright lights to protect themselves as light is all that fears the Morlocks.
