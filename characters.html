Characters

  • The Time Traveller
  • The unnamed man travels through time
  • He is an inventor who loves to speculate the future and the structures he sees
  • He lives in a suburb of London, Richmond, to be specific
  • The Narrator
  • Mr. Hillyer, he is a guest of the Time Traveller at a dinner
  • He is very curious and returns to investigate the following day of the first time travel
  • Weena
  • She is an Eloi, and though it is difficult to decipher the Eloi's genders the Time Traveller presumes she is female
  • The Time Traveller saves her from drowning and she quickly becomes his friend, similar to a pet
