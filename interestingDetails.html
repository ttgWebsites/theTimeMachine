Interesting Details

  • This novel was so fascinating and well received by many that it went on to be turned into two movies, several different comic versions, and inspired many other science fiction works of literature.
  • The author was the first person to use the term "time machine", and thus to this day it is the common term for a vehicle with capabilities of traveling through time, though none such exists.
  • Wells is also the author of the extremely famous science fiction novel War of the Worlds.
